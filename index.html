⚛️ What is Grover's Algorithm?
A quantum computer does not try passwords one by one. Grover's algorithm uses superposition to check all possibilities simultaneously, finding the answer in √N steps instead of N. For a billion combinations — the difference between 31 years and 1 second. This is why cryptographers recommend doubling key lengths in the post-quantum era.
